At The Flying Dane, we specialize in providing top-quality packaged food for regular passenger and private jet flights. Based in Billund, our strategic location and direct ramp access ensure swift order and delivery times, making us your reliable partner for inflight catering.

About Our Journey

Passion Meets Experience

We are a small team of entrepreneurs with extensive experience in the airport business. After years of working in the industry, we decided to start our own venture, focusing on what we know best: quality and taste in inflight catering.

Our Philosophy

At The Flying Dane, we believe that the journey to exceptional food begins with the eyes appreciating its beauty, the nose savoring its aroma, and the tongue experiencing its exquisite flavors. We strive in every dish, ensuring that each meal is a delight for all the senses.

Our Commitment

Our deep understanding of the industry's needs drives us to deliver the highest quality inflight catering. From traditional Danish delights to international cuisines, we are dedicated to providing a memorable dining experience for every passenger.
